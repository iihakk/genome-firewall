The firewall

Four gates in front of the model

01
Confidence
Asymmetric band, 0.35–0.65. Not centred on 0.5 — the two errors are not equally survivable.
02
Novelty
Unrecognised machinery blocks a susceptible call at any confidence.
03
Completeness
A sparse genome is more likely a bad assembly than a clean isolate.
04
Coherence
k=25 Jaccard neighbours. If they were 90% resistant and we are about to say susceptible — refuse.
